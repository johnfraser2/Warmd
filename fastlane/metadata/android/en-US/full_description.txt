The IPCC and COP21 told us that the global warming still can stay below +35°F/+2°C and leave a troubled but still habitable planet to futur generations. However we need to act fast.

This app allows you to calculate your carbon footprint, compare it with the average and get an idea on how you can improve it and why you should.
